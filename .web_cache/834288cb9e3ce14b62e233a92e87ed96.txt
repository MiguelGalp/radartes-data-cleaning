The funding program “LinzIMpORT” is intended to support artists and independent art and cultural initiatives with a connection to Linz with experimental art. LinzIMpORT projects aim to achieve the following effects: Positioning Linz as a modern cultural city with the priorities set out in the Linz Cultural Development Plan (KEP) Building networks and contacts between local and international art and culture scenes and creating added value for Linz art and culture activists sustainable impulses for a vibrant independent art and culture scene in Linz Promoting interdisciplinary artistic creation processes and enabling artistic work in Linz with artists who work outside of Austria.
