Encontros da Imagem is excited to announce that our annual portfolio reviews --- with a jury of internationally and nationally acclaimed professionals in the photography field --- are returning to the historic city of Braga in September 2025. This Award offers a chance to meet with recognized commissioners, gallerists, curators and publishers, to present your portfolio, promote your work and receive valuable feedback and advice. The 32 selected photographers will all give 10 minute public presentations, followed by 8 individual review appointments with the jury members, lasting 10 minutes each.
