One artist, or collaborative groups of artists will receive $75,000 to create compelling work that enrich the public art experience and celebrate Lincoln Road's dynamic cultural landscape. This initiative aims to inspire creativity, engage the community, and enhance the visual and cultural vibrancy of Lincoln Road through innovative art. Project Location: Lincoln Road Business Improvement District, Miami Beach, Florida 33139 between Washington Avenue and Alton Road. Project Timeline: Concept development and design phase: Deadline by June 18, 2025. Installation period: Between November 1-10th.
