The festival offers a platform for encounters between contemporary circus and visual art. In an open-air exhibition, you will have the opportunity and support to present your artistic work in front of an extraordinary backdrop between the circus tent and the stands to over 11,000 visitors and become part of the festival family. We are looking for works of art of all genres, such as sculpture, performance ( no circus acts ), painting, or photography, that can be installed outdoors on the festival grounds. Do you have an artistic work or a concept for a site-specific work that you would like to present on the festival grounds? Then send us your complete application using the application form (see below) and become part of the Berlin Circus Festival 2025! PERIOD * Exhibition: July 30 - August 10, 2025 * Exhibition opening: July 30, 2025 * Setup: July 25 - July 28 (earlier possible) * Dismantling + return transport: August 11 + 12, 2025 Eligibility Rewards Does this sound like something for you?
