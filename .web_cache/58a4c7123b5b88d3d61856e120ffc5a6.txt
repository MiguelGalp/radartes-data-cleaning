The work will be displayed in our West End Gallery from August 1 – October 4, 2025 . Artwork must be no larger than 5” x 7” and be wall mounted. All media will be considered, including paint, drawing, printmaking, mixed media, sculpture (wall-mounted), digital, and photography.
