The Eleventh International Triennial of Stage Poster-Sofia is fast approaching, and this is your opportunity to showcase your talent on a global stage. This unique event celebrates the artistry behind cultural posters, which are vital in promoting a wide range of creative performances and events. Whether you specialize in classical designs, cutting-edge experimental work, or a combination of styles, your poster could become part of this prestigious exhibition. The Triennial welcomes posters that promote an array of cultural activities, including theatre, opera, dance, musicals, circus acts, puppet shows, pantomime, concerts, festivals, video art, and visual performances. Whether your artistic approach is bold and experimental or refined and traditional, your creativity has a place here. Event Details The Eleventh International Triennial of Stage Poster-Sofia will be held in November/December 2025 in Sofia, Bulgaria. How to Participate Artists are invited to submit their poster designs created between April 30, 2022 , and May 30, 2025 . There are two exciting categories for entries: Printed Posters for Cultural Events: Showcase your work for theatre, film, music, or other artistic performances. Whether for a classical concert or an avant-garde multimedia show, your poster is welcome here. Posters on a Special Theme: This year's theme is "Literature is a Stage." Your designs may either be created for past events or specially prepared for this Triennial edition. Submission Guidelines Posters must be at least A2 size (420 x 594 mm) . Each participant can submit up to four posters . Digital versions of your work should be provided in A5 size , in .jpg , .eps , .tif , or .pdf format at 300 dpi resolution in CMYK mode. A completed application form and a brief CV must also accompany your submissions. Don't miss your chance to be part of this vibrant cultural celebration! We can't wait to see your inspiring creations.
