Loop staff and Guest Artist Laura Hyunjhee Kim will provide feedback along with fellow participants. Objective: To assist artists through private feedback sessions in a supportive small group setting, drawing on the time-honored tradition of collective art salons. Artists share information and insights with each other to foster a collaborative learning environment. Private Critique Club: Selected artists will participate in a 6-week critique club that meets 2x a week in the Loop metaverse on Mondays and Wednesdays from 12pm to 3pm EST. Please make sure this fits into your schedule . Participants must come to a minimum of 50% of meetings to be included in Exhibition. Exhibition: the residency will culminate in a collectively curated show by the artists. Support for creating this exhibition is provided by Loop staff and by Onland.io's XR Studio software. Anonymous Selection: Submissions will be reviewed anonymously by our juror, Laura Hyunjhee Kim, who will also attend some live critiques. The juror will not see the artist's name or any identifiying info. If included on a pdf, it will be removed. Critiques Begin: May 12th Critique End: June 19th Public Activation: June 19th - Oct. 5th Eligibility Rewards Does this sound like something for you?
