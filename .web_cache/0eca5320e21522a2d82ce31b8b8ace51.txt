The Bernd and Gisela Rosenheim Foundation was established in 1993 by the sculptor and painter Bernd Günther Rosenheim. It serves to promote contemporary fine art and awards an art prize with an associated exhibition. The winner will be announced at the opening of the exhibition on November 29, 2025. He/she will donate one of the exhibited works to the foundation, which will then be included in the foundation's collection. Eligibility Rewards Does this sound like something for you?
