Prizes and Awards COPIC AWARD 2025 Updated: 19 June 2025 We are excited to announce the launch of this year's “COPIC AWARD 2025,” a contest inviting all Copic users to submit their artwork! The COPIC AWARD is an online competition designed to connect Copic users worldwide. Now in its eighth edition, the previous 2024 edition received an incredible 3,639 submissions from 70 countries. There are no restrictions; we welcome a wide range of original artworks created using Copic products. Participation is free.
