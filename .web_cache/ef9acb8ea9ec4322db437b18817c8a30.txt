Undergraduate Admissions Apply Visit Get Help Graduate Admissions Apply Visit Get Help Thirdspace Residency Notification List 2026 THIRDSPACE: Emerging Artist Summer Intensive Program is a month-long residency program at OCAD University that brings together a dynamic group of interdisciplinary emerging artists, including those in the early stages of their careers and those embarking on new directions in their creative practices. Guided by an established Toronto-based, internationally recognized artist-in-residence, you will deepen and expand your work within a thematic framework. This immersive program offers unique opportunities to explore art, culture, and the environment in and around Toronto, and includes a month of open studio access and final exhibition showcasing the innovative work you develop throughout the program. In this residency, you will: Develop technical and conceptual skills Expand professional networks and collaborative practices Strengthen artistic practice through reflection and experimentation This program is for you if you are: A current undergraduate or graduate students A recent graduate A practicing artist in the early stages of your career A practicing artist exploring new directions in your practice. Sign up today to get notified as soon as registration opens. Current undergraduate and graduate students, as well as recent graduates and practicing artists are all welcome to apply. Questions?
